Test Subjects [TS]

> test subjects are, like the name suggests, the ones that will be used to test anomalies or do labor which has a high risk of killing EDCE personnel
> they know of their past, taken from prisons, either death row or without people that will miss them - this often makes them very volatile, so SCED is always allowed to use force
> most ethical rules don't apply to them
Apply Here

Security & Containment Enforcement Division [SCED]

> so far the most popular department, they are general facility guards that ensure everything goes smoothly without issues
> they are usually stationed at either checkpoints, TSC, or patrolling the hallways
> they have full permission to use lethal force if anything threatens the safety of other personnel
Apply Here

Specialized Counter-Anomaly Rapid Response & Enforcement Detachment [SCARRED]

> SCARRED are the equivalent of MTF, the only difference being is that they are permanently stationed at the site
> they are usually equipped with heavy weaponry, and they will respond to any disruptencies or threats in the facility as quick as possible
> rarely seen in TSC
Apply Here

Anomaly Containment & Neutralization Sciences [ACNS]

> they are the researchers that conduct tests and contain anomalies
> their primary objective is to find ways to neutralize anomalies
> they are not allowed to get past the stairs in TSC without a good reason
Apply Here

Health & Trauma Services [HTS]

> they are pretty much doctors and nurses
> they are the ones who give out amnestics
> they are allowed to conduct check-ups on personnel and give them medical assistance
> they are not allowed to get past the stairs in TSC without a good reason
Apply Here

Facility Engineering & Technical Services [FETS]

> they are the facility engineers who repair broken objects
> they are not allowed to get past the stairs in TSC without a good reason
Apply here

Board of Facility Administration [BFA]

> they are the facility administrators who supervise everything and make all decisions
> they are the ones who raise alarms, make announcements, etc
> they have full power and permission over everything
